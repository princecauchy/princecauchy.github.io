cookies

向浏览器中设置cookies.

读取cookies

req.cookies.remember读取名为remember的cookies变量.

保存cookies

req.body.remember 获取表单参数;(详见:body-parser)
res.cookie('remember', 1, { maxAge: minute }) 设置名为remember的cookies变量,生存时间为1分钟.

清除cookies

res.clearCookie('remember');清除名为remember的cookies变量.
